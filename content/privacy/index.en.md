**Jietnašiella**
Last updated: 2026-06-28

## Data Controller

UiT The Arctic University of Norway (UiT) / Divvun

Contact: <feedback@divvun.no>

## What Data We Collect

When you use Jietnašiella, we collect the following:

- **Voice recordings**: Audio recordings you voluntarily submit through the app.
- **Recording metadata**: Duration, sample rate, bit depth, number of audio
  channels, content type, and timestamp of each recording.
- **Device information**: Operating system name and version (e.g. "Android 14").
- **Client identifier**: A randomly generated anonymous ID (UUID) stored on your
  device. This is not linked to your name, email, or any account. It is used
  solely to group your donations and to allow you to delete your data.

## What We Do Not Collect

- We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information.
- We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
- We do not use cookies.
- We do not access your contacts, location, camera, or any data beyond
  microphone input.

## Why We Collect This Data

Your voice recordings are used to build speech corpora for language research and
the development of language technology. The metadata is collected to ensure the
technical quality of the recordings.

## Permissions

The app requests **microphone access** to record your voice. No recordings are
made without your explicit action. You may deny or revoke this permission at any
time through your device settings.

## Where Your Data Is Stored

Recordings and metadata are stored locally on your device until uploaded. Uploaded data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored in Microsoft Azure Blob Storage in the North Europe region (hosted in the EU/EEA). Access to the stored data is restricted to UiT staff with explicit permission.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Voice recordings and associated metadata may be shared
with research partners for the purpose of language research and language
technology development. Data is not shared with advertisers or other third
parties for commercial purposes.

## Data Retention and Deletion

To request deletion of your data, copy your client identifier from the app
(Details → Privacy → Copy Client ID) and send it to <feedback@divvun.no>. We
will delete all recordings associated with that identifier from our servers. You
can also delete recordings stored locally on your device by uninstalling the
app.

## Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly
collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected
in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
